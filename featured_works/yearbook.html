Yearbook
WebApp for Stanford Seniors in the Class of 2020 who didn't get to say goodbye to their classmates due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
> School Project
> 2020

When the COVID-19 pandemic abruptly interrupted our senior year, we found ourselves finishing college remotely, missing out on crucial in-person experiences. To preserve our memories and connections, we created "Yearbook," a dedicated website for the class of 2020. This platform allowed classmates to leave messages, share memories, and express affirmations. Our design goal was to create a low-pressure forum where everyone, not just close friends, could express appreciation and gratitude. Through extensive design research, wireframing, UI/UX design, product management, branding, and marketing, we brought this heartfelt project to life.
