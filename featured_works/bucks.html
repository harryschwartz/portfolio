NFT Campaign for the Milwaukee Bucks.
> Work Project
> 2022

The Milwaukee Bucks hired Mojito to build an exclusive web3 community centered around NFTs that granted VIP benefits to their owners.

As Mojito's Go-to-Market Manager, I led this project from concept to launch. I served as the Bucks' main point of contact, overseeing ideation, design, testing, and launch, while simultaneously managing our internal engineers to ensure successful execution on time and on budget.

The primary challenge was onboarding Bucks fans to web3, who for the most part were not crypto-native. The back-end lived on the blockchain, but we had to make sure the user experience on the front-end felt as familiar as possible. Instead of a crypto wallet, users claimed and managed their tokens through a traditional account on the Deer District All Access website. They logged in using email and password. No need for crypto. No need for Metamask.

On gameday, the NFT itself was your ticket, which was a novel utility in the industry at the time.

I traveled to Fiserv Forum for the launch and surprised some of the members with courtside tickets. They were elated!

Unfortunately, the project was halted after the market crashed, but it showcased innovative fan engagement strategies with a legacy web2 brand.
