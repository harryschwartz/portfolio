Built a study tool for MBA students looking to practice accounting with real 10-Ks.
> Hackathon Project
> 2024

I built this tool with three friends over the course of a weekend for UC Berkeley's biggest hackathon, Cal Hacks. We got wrecked on our accounting final. Our professor made a compelling point: you can't truly learn accounting without diving into real financial statements. To help ourselves and our classmates improve, we created a tool for practicing with actual 10-Ks.

Our tool helps business students develop practical skills by going beyond simplified textbook examples.
